WELCOME

ASB Capital assists our clients and associates access structured alternative investments by utilising our experience in asset backed securitisation bonds coupled with our network of experts in securitisable assets.

WHY

Complex or alternative projects seeking funding from the capital markets can be challenging for investors to analyse and fit to their investment criteria. Vanilla debt or equity instruments are often not appropriate or viable. Such projects are often also so unique that, when analysed in full detail, price is difficult to measure and risk diversification, within the investor’s existing portfolio, is difficult to achieve.

WHAT

Capital Arbitrage

By utilising our network of experts and constructing portfolios of carefully selected assets, we deliver value for clients by enabling access to structured investments with improved Price and Risk Arbitrage, or Capital Arbitrage, which have clearly defined terms, returns and exits.

HOW

Asset Backed Securitisation Bonds

ASB Capital acts as the Arranger to pool assets which meet our strict criteria, into a securitisation vehicle. In order to achieve the desired Capital Arbitrage levels for investors, we carefully analyse asset prices and quantify portfolio risks which are then modelled against various outcome scenarios. The securitisation vehicle then raises funds by way of the issuance of high yield bonds in order to provide uniformly defined terms, returns and exits for investors.

By utilising securitisation as a method of funding structuring, we are able to provide investors with a range of risk/reward opportunities to suit appetites, while also providing the Originators’ Clients with access to sources of capital at more attractive rates and terms than traditional sources may allow.
